Frequently Asked Questions

What is Otacon?
Otacon is a personal AI assistant that runs on your own computer. It's a simplified wrapper around OpenClaw that makes it incredibly easy for non-technical users to run their own AI.
Is it really free?
Yes! Otacon is 100% free and open-source (MIT license). However, the AI service you connect to (like OpenAI) may charge for usage. You can also use free alternatives through OpenRouter.
Do I need to know programming?
Not at all! Otacon is designed for everyone. Just download the installer, click "Start Otacon", and start chatting. No command line, no coding, no complex setup.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Otacon runs entirely on your computer. Your conversations and files are stored locally. The only data sent to the internet are the messages you send to the AI service (like ChatGPT).
What AI models can I use?
Otacon works with many AI models including GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Claude, and free models through OpenRouter. You can switch between them in the settings.
Can I use it offline?
You need internet to talk to the AI service, but your data and settings are stored locally. We're working on support for local AI models that work 100% offline.
